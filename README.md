## Description

Here are my projects for the undergraduate course *Topics in Quantum Physics*. The projects are scripts in Python written to solve problems in Quantum Mechanics. A reference point for these projects is Zettili's *Quantum Mechanics*.

![PQM_IntroImage](PQM_IntroImage.png)

## Assignment Descriptions
* **Assignment 1**: The 1st assignment is a computational study of the infinite square well using the analytic solution for the wavefunction. In the first part, the spatial parts of the wavefunction and their corresponding probability distributions are plotted. In the second part, the time evolution of a superposition of the eigenstates is animated.
* **Assignment 2**: The 2nd assigment is a study of the Larmor precession. In the first part, the Hamiltonian eigenvalue problem is solved numerically, followed by the diagonization of the Hamiltonian. In the second part, the previous results are used to implement a basis shift from the standard laboratory z-basis to the magnetic field's eigenbasis. This new basis is used to calculate the time evolution of the spin-state, and the probability to measure a specific $S_z$ value.
